# G4rden

**The free garden planner that grows with you.** → **[g4rden.com](https://g4rden.com/)**

G4rden is a free web garden planner with a retro pixel-art look. Tell it where you garden and it works out your climate, frost dates, and what to plant right now, from real weather data. Plan beds, track plants, get seasonal tasks. No app store. No account required to start.

G4rden works anywhere you can set a location. The US and Canada get the fullest support; everywhere else the core planner still runs, but some data sources don't cover every spot yet.

## Why G4rden

Most garden apps ask you to sign up before you've planted anything. G4rden is built the other way around:

- **Local-first:** your garden lives on your device. Cloud backup is optional.
- **Beds-first:** start with one patio bed; add a yard map later if you have one.
- **Honest estimates:** frost dates, harvest windows, and soil advice use ~ language because weather varies.
- **Beginner-friendly, grower-respectful:** approachable without dumbing down spacing, rotation, or variety data.

## What you get

**Planning**
- Drag-and-drop bed planner with companion planting nudges
- Polyculture guild templates (Three Sisters, salsa garden, pollinator patches, and more)
- Seed and spacing calculator
- Print and PDF export

**Climate**
- Growing zone and frost dates from 10 years of weather history for your location
- Interactive world map layered with soil, rainfall, temperature, and frost data
- 7-day weather forecast
- Frost checklist with actionable seasonal tasks
- Harvest window estimates from planting date

**Plant library**
- 230+ plants in pixel art and growing: veggies, herbs, flowers, houseplants, mushrooms
- 28 pepper varieties from bell to Carolina Reaper, with a heat finder
- Grow guides, companion planting, and rotation reminders
- Available in English, Spanish, French, Italian, and German

**Soil**
- Bed sizing, fill estimates, and bag counts (works everywhere)
- Yard soil lookup where survey data is available

**Tracking**
- My Plants home with care status chips
- Calendar with planting windows for your zone
- Care log and custom tasks

**Optional account**
- Works fully offline without signing in
- Cloud backup and sync when you want it
- Encrypted backup with a passphrase only you hold
- Shared gardens with family or friends

## Where you garden

**US & Canada:** full support. Climate, frost dates, and yard soil lookup all draw on regional survey data where available.

**Everywhere else:** the planner, plant library, bed tools, and frost dates still work. Your growing zone is estimated from local climate data on the USDA scale as a rough reference (it won't match a national system like Ireland's exactly). Yard soil lookup depends on global soil survey coverage and may not return data for every location. Bed calculators and manual soil entry always work.

If something looks off for your area, that's useful feedback: **hello@g4rden.com**
